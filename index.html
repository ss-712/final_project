ABOUT US

Welcome to Panarin Boxing Gym, where champions are made. Founded in 2018, our gym is dedicated to providing top-quality boxing training for all skill levels.

Our experienced trainers come from professional boxing backgrounds and are committed to helping you achieve your fitness goals, whether you're looking to compete or just get in shape.

At Panarin Boxing Gym, we believe in creating a supportive community where members push each other to be their best. Our state-of-the-art facilities and equipment ensure you'll have everything you need for an effective workout.
